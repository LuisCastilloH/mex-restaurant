Classical food

In essence, classical Mexican meals are those whole that have been eaten daily for years, no matter what season of the year we are. They are the meals that your great-great grandmother and grandfather would have eaten.

Classical foods focused on avoidance of modern and preparing and eating foods in the same manner that nourished our ancestors and kept them well. In essence if your great-great grandmother wouldn't recognize it, don't put it in your mouth.

Seasonal foods

Seasonality of food refers to the times of year when the harvest or the flavour of a given type food is at its peak. This is usually the time when the item is the freshest on the market. And each season characterizes for something.

Spring
Leafy greens like chard and arugula should each be in the market by the time spring begins.
Summer
It includes the explosion of over 75 different varieties of plums, peaches and nectarines.
Autumn
Much of what was available during summer's bounty will also bleed into most of autumn.
Winter
It is a well deserved respite for many farmers, yet it holds delicious fruits.
