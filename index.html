About Us

El Castillo's Restaurant is a new concept of food. Its main purpose is to deliver the traditional Mexican food to your place with just a preselection in its webpage. The current system includes only one restaurant but soon there will be more restaurants to cover the whole region.

El Castillo is located in Xalapa, Mexico. Well known for its authentic Mexican food, pico de gallo, fresh guacamole, Mexican cheese and margaritas. El Castillo has been proudly serving its loyal customers since 2015. We take genuine pride in serving our guests authentic Mexican dishes made with the finest ingredients available. You can find more information in the About us section.

Mexican markets

Mexican Markets are very important to us as a restaurant. Firstly because they are our food providers but also because they are part of the Mexican culture. We need as society not let them disappeared by the big multinational companies, otherwise we'd lost a big part of our culture.

That is why we maintain a close relationship with most of the local markets. We try to buy to all of them, it is important to mention that after a research we found more profitable to make business with the local markets. Go to the Mexican markets section to know more.
