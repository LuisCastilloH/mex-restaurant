About Us

El Castillo's Restaurant is a new concept of food. Its main purpose is to deliver the traditional Mexican food to your place with just a preselection in its webpage. The current system includes only one restaurant but soon there will be more restaurants to cover the whole region.

El Castillo is located in Xalapa, Mexico. Well known for its authentic Mexican food, pico de gallo, fresh guacamole, Mexican cheese and margaritas. El Castillo has been proudly serving its loyal customers since 2015. We take genuine pride in serving our guests authentic Mexican dishes made with the finest ingredients available.

History of the restaurant

El Castillo started to operate in January of 2015, with 4 employees. Jose as the head chef, Pedro (his nephew) as his assitant and finally Juan and Paco as the delivery guys. After one year the number of employees has increased, being in total 20 employees. This is due to the great customer demand

This Restaurant revolutionized the concept of Mexican Restaurant. Mainly because of these three concepts:

  • Fast way to order food
  • Less time of the cooking process
  • Takeaway service improved

Mission Statement and Guiding Principles

To establish El Castillo as the principal provider of Mexican food in a fast way while maintaining the best quality. To serve our customers and help our employees maximize their potential.

The following Guiding Principles help us measure our decisions:

  • Provide a great work environment and treat each other with respect and dignity
  • Apply the highest standards of excellence to the purchasing, preparation and delivery of our food.
  • Contribute positively to our community
  • Promote the Mexican cuisine
