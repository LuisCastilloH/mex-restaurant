Seasonal food

List of food available during Spring (each dish includes description and price in Mexican pesos). Below each meal you have the option to add the meal you want to your order request. When you are done adding your meals just hit order and you will get your food.

Seasonality of food refers to the times of year when the harvest or the flavour of a given type food is at its peak. This is usually the time when the item is the freshest on the market. And each season characterizes for something.

Spring

Spring brings many things with it: colors, aromas, warmth and joy. Spring for some reason brings good and pleasant emotions. Also in this season you can find the best fruits.
